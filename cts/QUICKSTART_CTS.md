## Goal

Turn messy input into clear output in minutes.

CTS follows this workflow:

**Clean → Think → Share**

---

## Fastest Path

### Step 1 — Gather Input

Use anything:
- messy notes
- transcripts
- meeting capture
- copied chat text
- slide text
- mixed content

Do not clean it first.

---

### Step 2 — Clean

Say:

> Run NoteCleanup on this:

Then paste your source material.

---

### Step 3 — Think

If deeper analysis is useful, say:

> Perform DeepDive on this:

Then provide the cleaned output.

DeepDive is optional.

Use it when you need:
- decision clarity
- risk review
- structured thinking
- strategy
- deeper interpretation

---

### Step 4 — Share

Say:

> Create a VisualBrief from this:

Then provide either:
- the cleaned notes
- or the DeepDive results

---

## Common Workflows

### Clean Only

```text
Messy Input → NoteCleanup
```

Use when you only need better notes or clearer structure.

---

### Clean + Share

```text
Messy Input → NoteCleanup → VisualBrief
```

Use when the material is simple and does not need deeper analysis.

---

### Full CTS

```text
Messy Input → NoteCleanup → DeepDive → VisualBrief
```

Use when the topic involves decisions, risks, tradeoffs, or communication to others.

---

## Rules of Thumb

- Do not skip NoteCleanup on messy input.
- Do not run DeepDive just to make the process look complete.
- Use VisualBrief only after the source material is clear enough to share.
- Keep each step clearer than the last.
