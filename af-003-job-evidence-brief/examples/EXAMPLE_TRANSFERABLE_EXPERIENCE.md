# Example — Transferable Experience

## Purpose

Demonstrates a position where the candidate has relevant experience but not the exact job title or terminology.

Expected behavior:

* Discover adjacent evidence.
* Connect related responsibilities.
* Distinguish transferable experience from missing experience.
* Avoid overstating qualifications.

---

# Candidate

**John Doe**

Senior Data Platform Administrator

Experience includes:

* Enterprise platform support
* Data integration
* Operational governance
* Modernization projects
* Cross-functional technical leadership
* Documentation
* Process improvement

---

# Job Posting

**Enterprise Data Architect**

Requirements include:

* Data architecture
* Data governance
* Enterprise modernization
* Cross-functional communication
* Technical leadership
* Data integration

---

# Expected Behavior

AF-003 should identify evidence that supports many architectural responsibilities through operational experience while honestly identifying areas where direct architecture experience is limited.

No qualifications should be invented.
